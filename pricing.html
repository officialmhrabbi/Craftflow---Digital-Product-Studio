Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most of our core design and development sprints take between 6 to 12 weeks, depending on the complexity of the product.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Absolutely. We love helping founders turn their vision into a tangible MVP that is ready for fundraising.

Will I own the source code and designs?

Yes, 100%. Once the project is complete and all payments are finalized, all intellectual property and assets are fully transferred to you.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

We do. We offer various post-launch maintenance and growth packages to ensure your product continues to perform optimally.
